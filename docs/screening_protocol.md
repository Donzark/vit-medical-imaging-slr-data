# Screening Protocol Summary

This document summarizes the systematic-review data workflow represented in the worksheet.

## Associated manuscript

**Vision Transformers in Medical Imaging: A Systematic Literature Review of Architectures, Training Strategies, Explainability, and Research Gaps**

## Review scope

The review examines Vision Transformer-based approaches in medical imaging, including architecture families, training strategies, explainability practices, and methodological/reproducibility gaps.

## Databases

The manuscript reports searches across:

- Scopus
- Web of Science
- PubMed
- IEEE Xplore
- ACM Digital Library

## Time window

The review covers studies published from **2020 to 2026**, corresponding to the period in which Vision Transformer-based models became prominent in medical image analysis.

## Search execution date

The final search was reported as executed on **16 March 2026**.

## Workflow represented in the worksheet

1. **Identification**  
   Records from the selected databases were exported and consolidated in `All_Extracted`.

2. **Deduplication**  
   Duplicate records were removed and retained records were placed in `Deduplicated`.

3. **Screening**  
   Title, abstract, and keyword screening decisions were recorded in `Screened`.

4. **Eligibility and quality assessment**  
   Full-text eligibility and quality-assessment criteria were recorded in `Eligibility`.

5. **Included studies**  
   The final included studies were recorded in `Included`.

6. **Data extraction and synthesis**  
   RQ-level extracted variables were coded in `Master Table`.

## Record counts

- Initial records: **683**
- Deduplicated/screened records: **482**
- Eligibility/QEA records: **297**
- Final included studies: **109**

## Quality-assessment categories

The worksheet includes eligibility/quality categories for:

- Objectives
- Methodology
- Limitations
- Findings

These categories support the review's inclusion and evidence-quality assessment.
